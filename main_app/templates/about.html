About.

MySomm. is a queryable web application that exists to help users quickly discover and make great decisions about wine. Users can learn about vintages and grape varietals, as well as tasting notes, food pairings and producers. Go to that party with confidence, knowing that you are carrying a bottle of wine everyone will love.
